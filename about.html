Building a Better Future Together

Welcome to the JCI Local Chapter. Since 1950, we have been a dedicated community of young leaders committed to creating sustainable, positive change in our city through action and collaboration.

Global Impact, Local Action

Connecting local leaders to a worldwide movement of active citizens creating positive change.

Junior Chamber International (JCI) is a global network of young active citizens aged 18 to 40 who are dedicated to creating positive change in their communities.

With over 150,000 members in 100+ countries, we provide development opportunities that empower young people to create sustainable impact. Our local chapter brings this global energy to our city, focusing on grassroots initiatives that matter to our neighbors.

Our Local Legacy

Founded over 70 years ago, our chapter has been at the forefront of local progress, developing generations of civic leaders.

1950

The Foundation

1950

Our chapter was founded by 12 visionaries who believed that young people could lead the post-war reconstruction of our local economy and civic life.

1975

Economic Catalyst

1975

Launched the landmark 'Young Entrepreneur' program, providing seed funding and mentorship to over 50 local startups, many of which are now industry leaders.

1998

Community Impact

1998

Inaugurated the 'City Green' initiative, planting over 10,000 trees and establishing three new public parks maintained by our youth volunteers.

2020

Digital Evolution

2020

Fully digitized our training curriculum and established a global mentorship network, connecting our local members with world-class experts.
